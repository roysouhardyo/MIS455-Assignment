Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Where is IUB located?

Independent University, Bangladesh Plot 16, Block B, Aftabuddin Ahmed Road Bashundhara Residential Area, Dhaka 1245 Bangladesh

What are the academic programs offered at IUB?

IUB offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines, including Business, Engineering, Social Sciences, Environmental Science, and more.

Does IUB offer scholarships?

Yes, IUB offers a variety of scholarships based on merit, financial need, and special categories like sports or cultural contributions.

What facilities are available on campus?

IUB offers a variety of facilities, including modern classrooms, laboratories, a central library, research centers, sports grounds, and more.

What clubs and organizations are available at IUB?

IUB has numerous student clubs and societies, including sports clubs, debate teams, cultural organizations, and technology groups.

Does IUB offer career counseling services?

Yes, IUB has a dedicated Career Services Office that assists students with job placements, internships, and career development.
