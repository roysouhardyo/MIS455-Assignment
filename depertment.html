Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) at IUB, under the School of Engineering, Technology & Sciences (SETS), is the largest, most dynamic and versatile department in the university.

Electrical and Electronic Engineering

Department of EEE is one of the largest and leading departments in IUB. The department strives to be at the forefront of electrical and electronic engineering education, research and innovation to address national and global challenges.

School of Liberal Arts and Social Sciences Global Studies and Governance

The Global Studies & Governance (GSG) department at IUB interprets global diversities both pragmatically and theoretically to shape socio-cultural, political-economic, interactive, and ecological policies
